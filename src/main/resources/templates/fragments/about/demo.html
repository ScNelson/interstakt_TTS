Demo

The following is a simple piece in Fmin that demonstrates the major components of Interstakt.

5 main sections (“scenes”)

  • Intro (Opening Theme in Lead Voice)
  • A (A or SlowA in Fmin [root key])
  • B (B or SlowB in Abmaj [relative major])
  • C (C or SlowC in Cmaj [major V])
  • Outro (Fast [Cmaj] or Slow [Fmin] Final Gesture)

4 Voices

  • Lead
  • Bass
  • Ambient (voice samples through effects)
  • Kick (kick drum)

The composition always contains the same material, but it plays back differently every time.

In order to demonstrate this, there are 4 different playthrough examples:

  • 2 Algorithmic (Will always play Intro – A – B – C – Outro when strictly algorithmic)
  • 2 Controller-Influenced (One button disrupts the algorithmic playthrough)
