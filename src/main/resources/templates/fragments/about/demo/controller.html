Demo - Controller

The controller examples focus on the Velocity control, and one button. There is still an algorithmic playthrough, but the button can disrupt it and/or cause the piece to play indefinitely.

The Velocity control is configured to only control Velocity in this example.

The button’s mappings are variable: they are dependent on what the current scene of the Score is playing. Whenever a new scene is triggered, then the button’s control scene is updated.

Possible Button Control Scenes:

  • Inactive – will not send any output

    (Occurs when the Intro and Outro scenes are active)

  • A – will trigger the A scene

    (Can occur when the C scene is active)

  • B – will trigger the B scene

    (Can occur when the A scene is active)

  • C – will trigger the C scene

    (Can occur when the B scene is active)

  • Kick_Fill – will trigger one of the Kick_Fill phrases in the Kick Voice

    Can occur when any of the A, B, or C scenes are active

    After a Kick_Fill phrase finishes playing, it will then randomly move the Score to the A, B, or C scenes. Therefore, the performer’s button presses can cause the piece to continue indefinitely, or skip scenes entirely.
