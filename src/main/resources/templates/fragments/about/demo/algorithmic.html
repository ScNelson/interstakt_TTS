Demo - Algorithmic

When the composition plays out strictly algorithmically, it will always go one route:

Intro – A (A or SlowA) – B (B or SlowB) – C (C or SlowC) – Outro

There are different random functions that determine the playout, so it will likely be a different playout every time:

  • Different route (could maneuver to either normal or “Slow” versions of next scene)
  • Different scene lengths (might play back longer during different playthroughs based on how randomized thresholds are or are not met)
